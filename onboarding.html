Program Onboarding

Every phase of the onboarding process with a new employee is important to the successful completion of their first year. With goals towards helping in creating a welcoming atmosphere and providing the guidance necessary for new hires to acclimate to the service organization, we’ve designed a series of useful online modules as checklists that provide a step-by-step guide for associates to navigate this process.

The checklist can be accessed as a certification course on myDevelopment so that the associates and their mangers can look at the progress made going through all the modules.
